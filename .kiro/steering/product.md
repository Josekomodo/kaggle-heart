# Product

Kaggle Playground Series Season 6, Episode 2 — Heart Disease Prediction.

Binary classification competition predicting the probability that a patient has heart disease (`Heart Disease`: Presence/Absence) from 13 clinical features. Data is synthetic, generated from the Cleveland Heart Disease dataset.

- Task: Binary classification (probabilistic output)
- Evaluation metric: ROC AUC
- Target column in train: `Heart Disease` (categorical: `Presence` / `Absence`)
- Submission: continuous probability [0, 1] of `Presence` per patient `id`
